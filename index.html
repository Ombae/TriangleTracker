Triangles

Triangles are very interesting shapes! Did you know that triangles are the most stable shapes!! Triangles are shapes with three sides, sometimes named base, height and hypotenuese. When we look at the behaviour of these Triangles we can categorise into different types.

Types of Triangles

There are three types of trianles as listed below

  1. Equilateral Triangle-all sides are equal.
  2. Isosceles Triangle-two sides are equal.
  3. Scalene Triangle-no side is equivalent to another.
